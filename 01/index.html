RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

The RO.ME demo is a music video and is also like a strange dream, swapping between eating beans, being in an empty city, being on a train, and then being in a desert and controlling (or becoming?) a flock of birds to beautiful music. The visuals are amazing, where some parts of the video are almost monochrome and others are very vibrant. Overall, it's just absolutely beautiful.

Technology: This demo uses WebGL, Three.js and JavaScript, and HTML allowing for the amazing 3D graphics to be seen on your own browser. 2D graphics are also used in ROME, like in the opening with the fire and the can of beans. The graphics are all rendered through the use of your video card. Three.js was the JavaScript library the creators used.

Comparison: https://persepolis.getty.edu/ Persepolis Reimagined isn’t a music video, instead it’s a look into a time passed. You get to see parts of Persepolis in the present and how it’s imagined to have looked in the past. Some text seen throughout the online tour is also translated and then recorded in English, which is a very interesting listen in my opinion. There’s also text shown when you stop at certain parts of the city, telling you about why a statue was there or why something was removed. You get to learn about how people lived in the city in the past, why it was built the way it was, and how things changed, which is so cool to me. There is music playing during it, but it’s not at all like the song in ROME, as there’s no lyrics. The graphics are very different from ROME’s, seeming more realistic in comparison as well.
