RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

Rome 3 Dreams of Black is an interactive movie made using webgl which shows a series of dreams that are animated in live time. It demonstrated the capabitlities of webgl and what you can now do on webbrowsers and it was revolutionary for its time in 2011

Technology: WEBGL
