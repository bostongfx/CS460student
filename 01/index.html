RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is a very cool webgl demo! It is an interactive movie that allows you to experience a dream world. Its technical aspects are very impressive, and the visuals are stunning. The demo is very well done and is a great example of the artistic possiblibities of webgl.



Technologies:

- WebGL

- Three.js

- Html

- Javascript

- Blink Engine (Chrome)



Comparison: Into The Storm

Into the storm has a different approach to story telling than ROME. Its goal is to tell a real story about an air force plane that crashed in the snow storm. Does not give the user as much freedom to explore and interact as ROME does.
