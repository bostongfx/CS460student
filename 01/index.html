RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is a very cool webgl demo! RO.ME allows the viewer (in the browser) to control the scene in front of them, during the interactive aspects, to "shape" how they want the world that they are in. We are able to move our mouse to control where the view walks, flys, or travels throughout the screen. RO.ME was created so that viewers could experience a new way to view a music video and it also showed that websites no longer have to be simple text and images but rather interative viewspaces.

Comparison: TODO! Link: Into the Storm
Into the Storm by the U.S Airforce brings viewers into a real life scenario regarding a rescue mission taking place during brutal winter storm conditions. While also using WebGL in order to convey a story, Into the Storm differes from RO.ME in its story telling and interative methods. RO.ME allows users to "craft" their own experiences , Into the Storm lets users follow a predestined path through the story, leading them through the experiences of the rescue team rather than crafting their own story path.
