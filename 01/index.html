RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is a film which uses webGL, it combines 2D graphics and 3D graphics to make an immersive story where the user is able to interact with the scenery. The impact of RO.ME was significant as it showcased the capabilities of webGL.What I found interesting was that I could see different perspectives of the scenery by moving my mouse. I could control the movement of the birds through my mouse, and I noticed new graphics popping up when they were flying low, which I thought was cool. Another interesting aspect of this film is that it was created with codes, it runs in the browser and does not require any plug-ins.
