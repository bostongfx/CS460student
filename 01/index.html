"3 Dreams of Black" is an interactive WebGL film developed by Chris Milk in partnership with Google. Released in 2011, it showcased the potential of WebGL by enabling high-quality 3D graphics directly in the browser without requiring additional plugins. The film provides not only stunning visuals but also allows users to interact with surreal environments and creatures. At the time, it represented a major breakthrough in web technology, demonstrating the exciting possibilities for creating immersive and interactive experiences on the web. - GPT4o
