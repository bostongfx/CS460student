RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME takes you flying around different enviornments with the ability to look around with your cursor, and even change the story by adding more animals and changing your path. It is especially impressive because it is one of the first major uses of WEBGL that could really show what web based graphics could do.

Technology: WebGL, HTML5, enabling hardware-accelerated 3d Graphics in web browser.

Comparison: Comparison website. This isn't a story in the same way that RO.ME is, but more of a menu/recipe finder that uses the WEBGL technology to be far more interactive and mind boggling. I enjoyed the loading of the long scroll that builds up.
