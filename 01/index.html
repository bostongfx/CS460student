RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

The RO.ME demo gives me nostalgia for playing my favourite game, the Last of Us on PlayStation. The soundtracks, the vibes, the open-world experiences, and the interations are heart-touching. I didn't feel amaze when the video said that it was created 13 years ago, because I have been playing great games since I was a kid. What surpried me was that this was not a game, it was a Chrome experiment that was created with WebGL. Great design, animation with 2D, 3D graphics.

Unlike the open-world experience in RO.ME, the WLDLGHT serves the main purpose of indicating the Japanese-architectural house. Therefore, the WLDLGHT experience only has a 180-degree front rotation. Also, while in RO.ME, the user can move the cursor in the desirable direction, the WLDLGHT can only have one path, which is to go straight to the main door. There is no controlled character in WLDLGHT like RO.ME, so there aren't any character interactions with objects in this experience.
