RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is an interactive web experience that takes users to a virtual world, blending music, art, and technology to create a visual story. It was released in 2011 and built using WebGL. The impact of RO.ME was significant, as it highlighted the power of WebGL to create real-time, interactive 3D experiences, combining visuals and music to run smoothly on a website without the need for any additional software. This demo lets users explore a virtual world by clicking and moving their mouse to view wide, panoramic scenes. The experience is divided into three dreams, all set to the song 'Black'. A dark scene where they move around to explore objects. A colorful bird flying through a dark sky with white clouds, transitioning to nature and modern cities. A desert landscape with flying birds, where users rotate their view in 360 degrees, move group of birds, and click to change the birdsâ€™ colors to black or pink.

Technology: WebGL, HTML5, JavaScript, Hardward-accelerated graphics, 3D Modelling, Audio Synchronization

Comparison: I chose this website, ChainGPT, to make a comparison between it and RO.ME. There are two biggest differences between these two websites. The concept of the two interactive websites is focused on different purposes. RO.ME focuses on telling three unique dreams, while the ChainGPT website introduces the topic of Blockchain AI. Another difference is that the ChainGPT website enables users to interact with a robot, manually scroll down, and seamlessly read and learn content. RO.ME allows users to interact with characters and the environment in real-time (like changing bird colors or view a full 360-degree).
