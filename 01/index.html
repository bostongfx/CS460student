RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is a web experience created to showcase multiple technologies and as the video for the song black which brought together many talent both in the tech word an the music industry. The web experience is not just a simple video but is a real time render of different graphics blended in with a video, all interactive.

The technology used to create RO.ME are mainly WebGL and Three.js with multiple layers of complexity such as GLSL for shading, mapping, depth of field and 3D mouse movements. Ofc HTML5 and a sprinkle of css.

Cloud castle is an immersive 3D world in the sky that is very similar to RO.ME in many ways, first the both use WebGL and they also share the 3D mouse movement which make everything more immersive together what I would call a parallax effect, they also use a lot of intractable 3D models and sounds. What I find different is that on Cloud Castle the user is more in charge of the narration than in RO.ME.
