RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is a very cool webgl demo! RO.ME is an interactive movie experience where the you are taken trhough a series of dream-like experiences where you can drag your cursor to place animal characters on the landscape. The movie starts in a city then you are transported to a icy landscape. Lastly, the viewer is transported to a desert landscape and you are flying as birds. The experience in 2011 set a new precendent and standard for online web experiences. Since then, webGL has enabled many creators to produce increasingly interactive web user experiences.

Technology: TODO! RO.ME utilizes WebGL which is a JavaScript extension that enables web browser clients to use the power of their GPU. It enabled the project to render real time interaction with 3D models that were designed in modeling software like Blender or Maya.
