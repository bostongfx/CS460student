RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME released in 2011 and is an awesome WebGL demo accompanied by some good music. RO.ME showcased the potential of WebGL for interactive storytellying and helped popularize the three.js library for 3D on web.

Technology: WebGL, THREE.js, and Google App Engine

Comparison: MSI EGG HUNT is another cool WebGL powered experience. Unlike RO.ME, Egg Hunt is a more straightforward gameplay loop driven experience, with a highscore system to keep players coming back.
