RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is an interactive 3D animated music video made for the browser. It covers three seperate dreams to showcase some of the different capabilities of WebGL. It also mixes in different types of visual media alongside the 3D graphics. RO.ME wa made 13 years ago, just a while after WebGL was released. This shows how powerful and versatile WebGL is. A lot of time has passed since then and WebGL has only been improving.

Technology: RO.ME uses HTML, JavaScript, WebGL, and the browser. WebGL utilizes the GPU to render the high quality 3D graphics.

Comparison: Cloud Castles
Cloud Castles is a lore and story introduction to an RPG. It provides small nodes to click on and examine different creatures, setpieces, and lore points for the game. While RO.ME was an experience that was "on rails" and had specific points it would take you, Cloud Castles gave more freedom and lets the user go through each option at their own pace, allowing the user the scroll back and forth from the different nodes.
