RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

It feels like we were watching a film and playing a game at a same time, our character is just keep moving forward and the film allows us to steer around in 360 degrees and when we reach the desert we can just freely move around.

Technology: TODO! This short film was using WebGL and it could steer itself through the narratives.The HTML5 canvas element of the WEBGL enables hardware-accelerated 3D graphics in the web browser.
