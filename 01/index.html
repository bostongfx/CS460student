RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

Three different dreams play out. Driving through a city desolated by decay. Riding a train alongside wildlife. Flying across the badlands, bringing life to all you see.

Technology: A base of HTML and Javascript, with WebGL Libraries to allow the browser to access the user's GPU.
This experiment was released in 2011, and has since inspired countless other projects, ranging from interactive narratives to data visualizations.

Comparison: J Dilla's Donuts pays tribute to a legend of the American Hip-Hop scene. It has humbler aims than RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK" did. While 3 Dreams aimed to show off the technology, Dilla's Donuts Shop uses it as an ordianry feature within the website. This shows the normalization of this technology - what was in 2011 a cutting-edge experiment was used a decade later as a normal tool meant to take back stage to the music being showcased.
